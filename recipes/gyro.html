Greek Chicken Gyros

Homemade chicken gyros

This recipe for homemade Chicken Gyros boasts huge flavor with just a few simple steps. Made with a blend of spices with the perfect balance of herbs, smokiness, warmth, and aroma, gyro night can be enjoyed in the comfort of your own home! You’ll be blown away with your very own creation.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Greek yogurt
  • Lemon juice
  • Red wine vinigar
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Spice (cumin, oregano, paprike, cinnamon)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Tzatziki or mayo
  • Pitta or flat breads

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken: Add the chicken and marinade ingredients to a large freezer bag. Squeeze to mix all the ingredients around and transfer the bag to the fridge. Marinate for 1-4 hours.
  2. Cook chicken: Preheat the oven to 425F. Equip a baking sheet with a roasting rack. Line the chicken on the rack and bake for 15-18 minutes, or until the edges begin to crisp. You can turn on the broiler for 2-3 minutes if you’d like more crispiness without drying the chicken out.
