Pepperoni and Honey Classic Pizza

Pepperoni, Jalapeno and Mozzerella drizzled with hot honey.

See the classics will never go out of style or taste for that matter. That’s why I’m a big believer in sticking to classics, but adding a bit of flare to make it modern. Case in point: Easy Pepperoni Pizza with Hot Honey. Simple, straightforward pizza with drizzle of the modern era thanks to hot honey. So, let’s dive in and make something classic, cheesy, and a bit spicy.

Ingredients

  • 225g Strong bread flour
  • 1 and 1/4 teaspoon of dried active yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 140ml warm water
  • Tomato reducation or passatta
  • Mozzerlla Cheese
  • Sliced Pepperoni
  • Hot honey
  • Jalapenos

Steps

  1. Mix together all ingredients in a bowl
  2. Bring into a ball then cover and leave to prove for 1 hour
  3. Stretch dough to shape by hand
  4. Top with tomato sauce
  5. Add toppings and drizzle with olive oil
  6. Bake in a pre-heated oven on a hot stone
  7. Bake for 6 minutes then remove
  8. Add hot honey to taste
